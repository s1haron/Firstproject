How To Cook Jollof Rice

Ingredients : 4 cups of rice ,1kg beef, fish,goat,meat,chicken,4 cups blended tomatoes,1 cup tomato paste,
2 onion bulbs nicely chopped ,chili pepper,salt to taste,1 tablespoon curry, 1 tablespoon thyme ,3 seasoning cubes,
1/2 tablespoon ginger, grated 1 clove garlic , 1 cup of vegetable oil.

Method Of Preparation

  • step 1: Season the meat with the spices and boil till it is soft
  • step 2: Fry or grill the meat.
  • step 3: Parboil the rice for about 8-10 minutes and wash it
  • step 4: Pour the vegetable oil into a pot
  • step 5: Put half of the onions and fry till it looks translucent
  • step 6: Pour in the blended tomatoes and paprika and then fry for about 15 minutes till it becomes dry, pour the stock into the tomato puree and cook for another 10 minutes
  • step 7: Pour the sauce into the rice till it just covers the surface of the rice in the pot.Add the remaining onions into the pot with rest of the spices .
  • step 8: Add salt and pepper to taste .Allow to cook till it dries and still grainy (not marshy.)
  • step 9: Serve with meat,egg,plantain,(anything of choice).

    • How To Bake Meatpie

    Ingredients; 1 Medium Potato peeled and cubed,1/2 pound ground beef,1/2 pound ground pork,1/2 clove garlic chopped,1/2 cup chopped onions,
    1/4 cup of water,1/2 teaspoon mustard powder,1/2 teaspoon salt,1/2 teaspoon dried thyme,1/4 teaspoon ground cloves,
    1 teaspoon salt,1/4 teaspoon grounded black pepper,1/4 teaspoon dried sage,1(15 ounce) package refrigerated pie crusts.

    Method of Preparation

  • step 1: Preheat the oven to 425 degree (220 degree c),place the potato in a sauce pan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil and cook until tender , about 5 minutes .Drain, mash and set aside .
  • step 2: Meanwhile, crumble the ground beef and pork into a large pan and add the garlic ,onion and water.
  • step 3:Season with mustard powder, thyme, cloves and salt.
  • step 4: Cook over medium heat ,stirring until the meat is evenly browned .
  • step 5: Remove from the heat and mix in the mashed potatoes
  • step 6: Place one of the pie crusts into inch pie plate.
  • step 7:Fill with the meat mixture ,then top with the other pie crust.
  • step 8:Prick the top crust a few times with a knife to vent steam.
  • step 9:Crimp around the edges of the pies crust with aluminium foil.
  • Bake for 25 minutes in the preheated oven ,or until the crust has browned.
  • Serve by itself ,or with a beef gravy.

    vegetable (precisely bitter leaf),1 Cup of Egusi (melon should be grounded),1 cube of maggi ,1 teacup of palm oil, 2 cups of water, 1 teaspoon of salt.

    Method of Preparation

  • step 1: Get a clean pot place on the burner to heat and add a big spoon of palm oil in it.
  • step 2: Allow the palm oil to heat then add the remaining sliced onion and stir so as to avoid burning.
  • step 3: Add the ground egusi and stir fry until it starts sticking to the bottom of the pot
  • step 4: Add the meat or fish stock water to the pot
  • step 5: You can add water if you dont like your soup too thick.Allow to boil for 15 minutes then the meat, stock fish and allow to boil.
  • step 6: Add crayfish, maggi cube,grounded cameroonian pepper and the dry fish,stir and allow to cook for a while .Take a small spoo nand check to taste if there is enough salt and pepper .Add the shredded ugwu leaves(or bitter leaf) and allow to cook for a minute.     • How To Make Zobo Drink

    Ingredients: 1 bowl of dried zobo leaves , 1 teaspoon ginger ,flavors, sweeteners or sugar, garlic, lime or lemon juice(optional),pineapple peels or juice.

    Method of Preparation

  • step 1: Wash dried zobo leaves properly in ordinary water to get rid of sand and particles
  • step 2:Put the zobo in a cooking pot. Add moderate amount of water and allow to boil for about 25 minutes.
  • step 3:Add the grounded ginger and garlic ,the pineapple and any other flavor that you want to use.
  • Put in bottles and chill immediatelt to prevent fermentation
  • step 5: serve chilled ,if too thick dilute with water before serving.
